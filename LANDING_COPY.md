### FAQ
1.  **Is my data private?**
    We don't ask for API keys or browser access. You manually enter tool names and seat counts. We only persist the aggregate results to generate your shareable report.
2.  **How accurate is the pricing?**
    We strictly track the 8 highest-burn frontier tools (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Cursor, Copilot, Windsurf, Claude, and ChatGPT). By focusing exclusively on the heavy hitters, we can maintain 100% accurate, week-to-week pricing logic based on public tiers and known Enterprise contract floors.
3.  **Why is this tool free?**
    Lumen is a lead-generation tool for Credex. If we find >$500/mo in savings, we hope you'll book a call with our procurement experts to handle the negotiations for you.
4.  **Do you support custom Enterprise contracts?**
    Yes. Instead of guessing hidden "Contact Sales" tiers, our engine uses your actual inputted monthly spend to calculate your true cost-per-seat. We then mathematically compare that ground truth against publicly available tiers to find downgrade opportunities.
5.  **What if my stack is already optimized?**
    Great! Save your report and we'll notify you only when a tool in your stack changes its pricing or introduces a more efficient tier.
