# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM)

### Target User
*   **Segment:** Pre-Series A Technical Founders and Engineering Managers.
*   **Company Size:** 10–50 employees.
*   **Pain Point:** Scaling infrastructure costs outpacing revenue growth; unmanaged "shadow AI" spend.

### Search Intent (The "Bill Shock" Queries)
*   "why is my anthropic bill so high"
*   "github copilot business vs enterprise pricing comparison"
*   "reduce openai api costs for data processing"
*   "cursor business vs vscode cost benefit"
*   "is chatgpt team worth it for startups"

### Hangouts (Niche Communities)
*   **YC Startup School Forum:** "Technical Implementation" and "Cost Optimization" threads.
*   **Reddit:** `r/SaaS`, `r/ChatGPTCoding`, `r/LocalLLaMA` (for teams looking to exit high API costs).
*   **Slack/Discord:** "LlamaIndex" or "LangChain" community "production" channels.
*   **X (Twitter):** Lists of "YC W24/S24" founders.

### First 100 Users ($0 Budget)
*   **GitHub Repo Scanning:** Search GitHub for recently active open-source projects using langchain or openai in their package.json. DM the maintainers: "Noticed you're shipping heavy AI features. If your API bill is spiking, I built a local calculator to optimize token spend across models. No login: [Link]." launches.
*   **Cold DM Strategy:** DM founders on X/LinkedIn: "Saw your [Tool Name] launch. If you're hitting $1k+/mo in OpenAI/Anthropic burn, ran this auditor to cut my own bill by 40%. No login: [Link]."
*   **Build in Public:** Post weekly "Average Savings Found" charts to `r/SaaS` to drive organic curiosity.

### Unfair Distribution Channel
*   **Incubator Networks:** Partner with University-backed incubators (e.g., Stanford SSE, Berkeley SkyDeck alumni groups).
*   **ACM Chapters:** Distribute as a "financial hygiene" tool for collegiate hackathon winners who receive AWS/Azure credits but hit cost ceilings quickly.

### Week 1 Traction Targets
*   **Unique Audits:** 50
*   **Viral Shares:** 10 (Reports shared via `/r/[id]`)
*   **Consultation Requests:** 2
*   **Identified Savings:** >$5,000 total MRR
