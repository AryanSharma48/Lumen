# Go-To-Market Strategy (GTM)

### The Target User
The exact target user is a **Pre-Series A Engineering Manager or Technical Co-founder** at a startup with 10–50 employees. At this stage, they have just raised a Seed round, hired a small team of developers, and are transitioning from "build at all costs" to "why are we burning so much cash?" They are experiencing the pain of decentralized, unmanaged "shadow AI" spend where individual developers are expensing different Copilot, Cursor, and ChatGPT Plus subscriptions.

### The Trigger (Search & Scroll)
They are looking for this tool immediately after opening their end-of-month Stripe or AWS billing dashboard and experiencing "bill shock." Right before searching, they are actively Googling queries like:
* "why is my anthropic api bill so high"
* "github copilot business vs enterprise pricing comparison"
* "reduce openai api costs for bulk data processing"
* "cursor business vs vscode cost benefit"
* "is chatgpt team worth it for startups or stick to individual"

### Niche Hangouts
These users do not hang out in generic entrepreneurial forums; they are in technical trenches:
* **Forums:** YC Startup School Forum (specifically "Technical Implementation" and "Cost Optimization" threads).
* **Reddit:** `r/SaaS` for business logic, `r/ChatGPTCoding` for tooling, and `r/LocalLLaMA` (specifically founders looking to exit high cloud API costs by running local models).
* **Slack/Discord:** Production-focused channels within the LangChain or LlamaIndex community servers.
* **X (Twitter):** Lists tracking "YC W24/S24" founders and engineering leads.
